1.sorting is the process of rearranging the items in a collection(1.e. an array),
so that the items are in kind of order.
[eg:
    1.sorting numbers from smallest to largest,
    2.sorting names alphabetically
    3.sorting movies based on release year etc...]

::Bubble sorting
    - a sorting algorithm where the largest value bubble at the top!


Big O
time complexity

best case:O(n)  Average Case:O(n^2) Worst Case:O(n^2)

space complexity :O(1)
